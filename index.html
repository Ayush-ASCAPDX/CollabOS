// faq
Questions.
Answered.
Is ColabOS free to use? +
Yes — free forever for individuals. You can post projects, find collaborators, and build your reputation at no cost. We only charge companies who want to access our verified talent pool and post bounty projects.
How does revenue splitting actually work? +
Before a project starts, all collaborators agree on contribution percentages. When the project earns money — through sales, freelance work, or any other means — the platform automatically splits and distributes earnings according to the agreed percentages. Transparent, automatic, no disputes.
How is this different from Fiverr or Upwork? +
Fiverr and Upwork are for hiring freelancers to do tasks. ColabOS is for finding co-builders to build something together — where everyone has skin in the game. You're not hiring someone, you're finding a partner who shares in the outcome and the rewards.
Who can join ColabOS? +
Anyone who builds things — developers, designers, creators, writers, marketers, founders, students. If you have a skill and want to build something real with others, ColabOS is for you. There are no age or experience requirements.
When does ColabOS launch? +
We're targeting a public beta in Month 3 of our roadmap. Waitlist members get early access before the public launch and will have priority access to the best projects and collaborators on the platform.
Can I use ColabOS if I'm a student? +
Absolutely — students are one of our most important user groups. ColabOS lets you build real projects, gain real reputation, and create a portfolio of shipped work before you graduate. Your ColabOS profile can matter more than your degree.
