// our story
Built by a builder.
For builders.
ColabOS was born from a simple frustration: why is it so hard to find the right person to build with? We're fixing that.
The problem
we're solving.

Every day, thousands of talented developers, designers, and creators sit alone — with skills, ideas, and ambition — but no one to build with. Meanwhile, founders with vision can't find technical co-builders. Creators with audiences can't bring their ideas to life.

The existing solutions — LinkedIn, Upwork, Discord servers, random Twitter DMs — are all broken for this problem. They're either too formal, too transactional, or too chaotic.

ColabOS is the platform we wished existed when we were trying to build our first project. A place where your reputation is built on what you've actually shipped, not what you claim on a resume. Where collaboration is the default, not the exception.

🌍
Geography is not destiny
The best collaborator for your project might be in a different continent. We make global collaboration as easy as a local coffee meeting.
⚡
Proof over promise
Anyone can say they're a great developer. On ColabOS, you prove it through the work you've actually shipped, not the words on your profile.
🤝
Shared skin in the game
The best collaborations happen when everyone has something at stake. Our revenue splitting system aligns incentives from day one.
