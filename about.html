About Us

Building the Future of Team Formation

We believe every great idea deserves the right team. CollabOS connects visionary founders with skilled builders to transform ideas into reality.

Our Mission

Empowering Founders to Build Together

Too many great ideas never see the light of day because founders struggle to find the right teammates. We started CollabOS to solve this fundamental problem in the startup ecosystem.

Our mission is simple: make it effortless for founders to find, connect with, and start building alongside committed co-founders and team members who share their vision and drive.

Our Story

From Frustration to Solution

CollabOS was born out of our own frustration. As founders ourselves, we spent months searching for the right co-founders and team members. We tried LinkedIn, Twitter, Discord communities, and countless networking events.

Nothing worked. The people we found were either not serious, lacked the skills we needed, or simply disappeared after the first conversation. We knew there had to be a better way.

So we built CollabOS - a platform designed specifically for serious builders who want to create something meaningful. Every feature is designed to filter out the noise and connect you with people who are just as committed as you are.
